Distance Education Learning Model

The perfect balance of campus engagement and virtual flexibility

In-Campus Days

Two days per week of in-person learning at your nearest AMA campus. Engage with professors, collaborate with peers, and access campus facilities.

Virtual Days

Three days per week of virtual classes from any location. Access lectures, submit assignments, and participate in discussions through our advanced learning management platform.

Structured Flexibility

Maintain academic rigor while enjoying the convenience of studying near home. Perfect for students who want quality education without relocation.

Cost Savings

Save significantly on education costs with reduced commuting expenses, no need for dormitory fees, and lower overall campus-related costs while maintaining the same quality education.

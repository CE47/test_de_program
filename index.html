How Distance Education Works

Our streamlined process makes virtual learning simple and effective

1

Apply Online

Complete our simple application form and submit required documents digitally. No entrance exams required.

2

Receive login credentials for our state-of-the-art learning management system with all your course materials.

3

4

Receive Support
